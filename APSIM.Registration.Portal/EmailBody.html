Thank you for registering for access to the Agricultural Production Systems sIMulator (APSIM) Software. APSIM is available free for non-commercial, public good, R&D and educational use under the APSIM Community Source Framework.

In exchange for free access to APSIM, licensed users may develop improvements and in turn relinquish ownership of these improvements to the AI. At its discretion, the AI may adopt the improvements and include these in APSIM. Modifications to APSIM must be submitted to the AI and be subject to the Reference Panel evaluation and approval process before inclusion into official APSIM releases. The AI RP utilises the APSIM Initiative GitHub’s Organisation for approving all software changes and development activities dealing with APSIM. We encourage APSIM users and developers to engage with other APSIM developers via GitHub. You are able to review the repository by clicking on the link above.
